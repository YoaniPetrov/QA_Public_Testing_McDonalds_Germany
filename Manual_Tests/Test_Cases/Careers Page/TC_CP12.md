Title : Job opportunities page - Direct links (Duales Studium).

Description : Validate that the Job opportunities page's direct links redirect to offers page.

Steps :
1. Open https://www.mcdonalds.com/de/de-de.html
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page (Home Page)
3. Press on Careers (Karriere) link. / Alternatively: Open https://karriere.mcdonalds.de/ directly.
4. Click on "Möglichkeiten im Restaurant" from the top of the page. / Alternatively : Open https://karriere.mcdonalds.de/moeglichkeiten-im-restaurant/ directly.
5. Scroll down until "Dein Einstieg bei McDonald’s" banner with Filter options is visible.
6. Click on "Ausbildung & Duales Studium"
7. Click on @Duales Studium's "Im Restaurant"


Expected result : The button redirects to the job offers page with pre-aplied filters.
